Christine Huang


Full Stack Web Developer | Artist | Art Educator



I am a Software Engineer with a background in art education. I enjoy using front-end and back-end technology to develop user-friendly applications. My passion lies in using logical and creative thinking in my projects.

I have always been fascinated with creating art out of materials or resources that appear insignificant; for example, transforming a sheet of fabric into a stylish outfit or using colored paints to create emotion-provoking artworks.

I view software engineering with the same fascination. Stringing seemingly mundane letters and symbols together to form a functional application is a deeply-rewarding process. I hope to use my passion to develop applications that will help others improve their daily lives.
